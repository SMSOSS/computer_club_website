Introducing "tiferet"!

2022/03/21 - 5-Minute read

Imagine if takeaways were easier

The idea came to us one day in December 2021, when we were buying takeaways from a fast food restaurant. We wondered, what if someone could order food from an app by a takeaway service, and the food would get delivered to us?

However, this would be proven impossible as the school does not allow any type of takeaway delivery to enter. If the school doesnâ€™t allow such service, then we had to come up with an alternative on our own.

This is when the idea sparkled. Why not create a service that allows users to order takeaways, and the deliverers can put it in a location where direct contact could be avoided? This way, we can reduce the spread of the coronavirus, and, most importantly, order takeaways at school.

After a few discussions at the dining table, we came up with a concept. A website where users can order food, shops can keep track of pending orders and delivery men can do their job, interlinked with a whole system of lockers to store food, accessible to both users and deliverymen.
